Custom Formulations

Nobody’s skin is the same, so why are companies treating their patients and consumers as if they are? We are here to listen to your needs and help you create a formula that is truly unique. With expertise of with over 40 years of natural product chemistry, dermatology, and formulations; our dedicated Research and Development team will guide you through an easy process of creating your own products to integrate into your company or personal brand.

ur goal is to help you to create natural, safe, and clinically proven products. Bulk and custom manufacturing minimums begin at 18 liters or 600 (30 ml) units for most products. Custom products may be sold in bulk packaging or filled in a number of luxury packaging options. We can also work with client supplied packaging should you purchase directly from your own suppliers to integrate into an existing brand or skincare line.

Our products are manufactured in accordance with GMPC with reference to: ISO2217:2007(E) – Cosmetics – guidelines on good manufacturing practices.
